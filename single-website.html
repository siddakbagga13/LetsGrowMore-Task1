The Mission of
Our Tours & Travel

Finding an agent who lives in your destination of choice is often a
great (and in some cases, very affordable) idea, but be sure to vet
them before you send any money. You should always ask the agent
for client references who you can reach out to directly to ask about
their experience.

Some agents will only work with clients who are willing to spend a
minimum amount per day—it’s an industry metric that most agents
use to determine the total cost of a trip. Stating your budget upfront
will give your consultant some parameters to work with.
